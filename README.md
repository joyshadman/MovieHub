# 🎥 MovieHub

### Your Ultimate Cinematic Discovery Experience.

**[Live Preview](https://movie-hub-sigma-five.vercel.app/)** |

---

## 🌟 Overview

MovieHub is a premium, Apple TV-style streaming discovery platform designed with a sleek "Glassmorphism" UI. It provides users with an immersive way to browse trending movies, search for their favorite titles, and manage a personalized watchlist with fluid animations.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🎬 Immersive Browsing

* **Apple-Style Parallax:** Experience smooth, high-end scroll effects and ambient background blurs that change as you navigate.
* **Dynamic Hero Section:** Featured content with auto-playing highlights and quick action buttons.
* **Categorized Discovery:** Browse curated rows including **Trending, Action, Comedy, Thriller, Horror, Bollywood, and Romance**.

### 🔍 Powerful Search & Details

* **Instant Search:** Find any movie or TV show across a massive database in real-time.
* **In-Depth Info:** View high-resolution posters, synopses, ratings, and release dates with a single click.
* **Animated Overlays:** Beautifully transitioned modals for movie details and video playback.

### 👤 Personalized Experience

* **Continue Watching:** Automatically tracks your history so you can pick up exactly where you left off.
* **My List:** A dedicated space to save movies you want to watch later.
* **Real-time Sync:** Powered by Firebase to ensure your list and history are available across any device instantly.

---

## 🎨 Design Philosophy

* **Glassmorphic UI:** Transparent, frosted-glass elements for a modern, high-end feel.
* **Motion First:** Every interaction is powered by `framer-motion` for meaningful, non-distracting animations.
* **Dark Mode Native:** A deep obsidian-black aesthetic optimized for late-night binge-watching and OLED displays.

---

## 🛠️ Built With

* **Frontend:** [React.js](https://reactjs.org/) & [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com/)
* **Animations:** [Framer Motion](https://www.framer.com/motion/)
* **Database & Auth:** [Firebase](https://firebase.google.com/) (Firestore & Authentication)
* **API:** [The Movie Database (TMDB)](https://www.themoviedb.org/documentation/api)
* **Routing:** [React Router](https://reactrouter.com/)

---

## 🚀 What Can You Do?

1. **Explore:** Scroll through the home page to see what's trending globally or in specific genres like Bollywood.
2. **Search:** Use the glass-styled search bar to find specific titles.
3. **Watchlist:** Click on any movie to add it to your personal "My List".
4. **Resume:** Check the "Continue Watching" row to see your recently viewed items.
5. **Watch:** Experience seamless video playback directly within the app overlay.
